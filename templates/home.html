The WPI CAVE is an indoor bouldering wall under Harrington Auditorium managed by WPI’s Outing Club. Originally an on-campus IQP project, the CAVE has become one of the most popular locations on campus for students. The CAVE consists of many bouldering routes of various difficulties that have been set by WPI students, alumni, and other local route setters. Here, WPI students of all skill levels can spend time getting to know other rock climbers on campus while getting in a good workout. The CAVE is open to all WPI students and faculty Mon-Fri from 7pm-10pm and on Sunday from 8:30-11pm. Additionally, private events can be booked in the CAVE through the WPI Outing Club. WPI’s Outing Club also hosts various events and climbing competitions here throughout the year.
